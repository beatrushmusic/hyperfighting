SP-Slidekick Support

Ultimate MIDI Control for your Roland SP-404MK2

Getting Started

SP-Slidekick is designed to be plug-and-play. To get started:

  • Connect your iPhone or iPad to your Roland SP-404MK2 using a high-quality USB-C to USB-C (or Lightning to USB-C) cable.
  • Ensure your SP-404MK2 is powered on.
  • Launch the SP-Slidekick app. It will automatically attempt to detect and connect to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

The app says "Disconnected". What should I do?

Ensure your cable supports data transfer (some charging cables do not). Unplug the cable, wait 2 seconds, and plug it back in. Also, verify that your SP-404MK2 is not in a menu that disables MIDI communication (like USB Mass Storage mode).

How do I change the effect assigned to a button?

Long-press on any effect button to open the effect selection menu. From there, you can choose from all available SP-404MK2 internal effects.

Can I use this with Bluetooth MIDI?

SP-Slidekick is optimized for low-latency wired connections via USB-C to ensure the most responsive performance for live use. Bluetooth MIDI is currently not supported.

How do I save my layout?

The app automatically saves your slider positions and component labels locally on your device. You can also use the Snapshots feature to save specific bus configurations for instant recall.
