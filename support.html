Common Troubleshooting

MIDI Connection Issues

  • Ensure your SP-404MK2 is running the latest firmware (v4.04 or higher recommended).
  • Verify that your device is connected via a data-capable USB-C cable or a compatible MIDI interface.
  • On Android, ensure you have granted MIDI permissions when prompted.
  • If the device isn't found automatically, use the "MIDI Setup" menu in settings to select your port manually.

App Layout

  • Use Edit Mode (pencil icon) to add, move, or resize your controls.
  • Double-tap the screen to toggle the Bus Console sidebar.

Privacy Policy

SP-404 SlideKick does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. All project layouts and settings are stored locally on your device.
